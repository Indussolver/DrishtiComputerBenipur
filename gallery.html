Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you start your journey.

1. What courses do you offer?

We offer ADCA, Tally with GST, MS Excel, and basic computer courses.

2. Do I need prior computer knowledge?

No, our courses are designed for complete beginners as well as advanced learners.

3. What is the duration of the courses?

Course duration varies from 3 months to 1 year depending on the program.

4. Will I get a certificate?

Yes, we provide a certificate after successful completion of the course.

5. Do you provide practical training?

Yes, we focus on hands-on practical training for real-world skills.

6. What are the class timings?

We offer flexible timings to suit students and working individuals.

7. What are the fees for courses?

Our fees are highly affordable. Please contact us for detailed information on specific courses.

8. Where is the institute located?

We are located right here in Benipur and easily accessible for all local students.

9. Do you provide job support?

Yes, we guide students for career opportunities and provide basic job support.

10. How can I enroll?

You can visit our institute directly or contact us via phone or WhatsApp to enroll today.
