Founder's Message

Guiding You Towards a Better Future

“

At Drishti Computer Benipur, our goal is simple — to make computer education easy, practical, and accessible for every student. We believe that in today’s world, computer skills are not optional, they are essential for growth and career success.

We started this institute with a vision to help students from all backgrounds learn real-world skills like ADCA, Tally, and MS Excel in a simple and friendly environment. Our focus is not just on theory, but on hands-on training so that students gain confidence and can apply their knowledge in real life.

We are committed to guiding every student step by step and helping them build a better future through quality education.

Rahul Jha Founder, Drishti Computer Benipur
