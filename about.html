Institute Introduction

About Drishti Computer Benipur

Drishti Computer Benipur is a dedicated computer training institute focused on providing quality education and practical skills to students of all levels. Our aim is to make computer learning simple, effective, and accessible for everyone, whether you are a beginner or looking to upgrade your skills.

We offer a range of courses including ADCA, Tally with GST, MS Excel, and basic computer training, designed to meet the needs of students, job seekers, and working professionals. Our teaching approach emphasizes hands-on practice, real-life applications, and step-by-step guidance.

At Drishti Computer Benipur, we are committed to helping students build confidence, improve their knowledge, and prepare for better career opportunities in today’s digital world.
